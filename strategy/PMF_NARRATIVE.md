# VoiceTicket PMF Narrative (v1.0)
*Last Updated: 2026-03-07*

## 1. Target Customer
**The "Solo-Plus" Developer:** Founders/Builders with limited time (day jobs/agencies) who are the most sensitive to administrative overhead.

## 2. The Underserved Need
**Context-Switching Recovery:** Eliminating the 15-minute productivity gap between discovering a bug and successfully documenting it in Jira.

## 3. Value Proposition: "Velocity Over Everything"
- **Hook:** Record for 30s, get a Jira ticket in 60s.
- **Data Support:** Admin waste consumes **25% of team capacity**. We reclaim that 25% for building.

## 4. The Step Change (The PMF Moment)
The transition from "I need to remember to ticket that" to "It's already in the backlog." 

## 5. Monetization Strategy
**High-Volume/Low-Touch:** $25/mo flat fee for small teams (1-5 people). Validated against Linear/Trello ($50/mo for 5 seats).
